Niche E-Commerce App

Summary

An acquaintance is considering launching an e-commerce app targeting an underserved group of buyers and sellers. He approached me for consultation on how to proceed.

After a discussion about his proposed app, the target demographic, the competitive landscape, and his desired launch date, I recommended trying to define the feature set of the minimum viable product. It would be appropriate to begin work on mockups and prototypes once a rough feature set was established.

I created a mind map with necessary and potential features for his app and shared it with him as a first step toward defining the minimum viable product.
