Summary

I started as an iOS developer at Citi FinTech in May 2018. Due to a shortage of backend developers on my team, I have split my time 50/50 between iOS development in Swift and microservices development in Java since February 2019.

On the microservices side, I am responsible for the endpoint that generates those three dashboard tiles.
