Citi FinTech

Summary

I worked an iOS developer at Citi FinTech from May 2018 to July 2019. Due to a shortage of backend developers on my team, I split my time 50/50 between iOS development in Swift and microservices development in Java from February to May 2019.

Details

I worked on the Insights team at Citi FinTech. On the iOS side, I added features and fixed bugs on the Weekly Spending Summary, All Your Bills, and Monthly Overview dashboard tiles and associated screens. On the microservices side, I was responsible for the endpoint that generated those three dashboard tiles.

Highlights

  • I developed a miniature domain-specific language to make a powerful but complex Citi networking API easier to use
  • I had a reputation among the iOS developers as a thorough but fair code reviewer
